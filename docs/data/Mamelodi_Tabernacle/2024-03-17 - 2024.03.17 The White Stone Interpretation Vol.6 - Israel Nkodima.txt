The purpose of this sermon was to show the audience about the White stone interpretation.

You have heard from your theophany, you are not going to hear. Before God put you in the body there was a theophany, which is the body subject to the word of God. Without Him we can do nothing. When God speak about a man, He doesn't count the years.

The theophany is the only one that can keep the word of God, because it is the word. 

Abraham when looking for a city he was not just looking but started walking to the city. Elijah knowing the chariot was coming he started walking towards the chariot. Enoch seeing that the rapture is coming he started walking. The sign that Enoch was going was the pyramid. The sign that the children of God are going is the ministry of the pyramid.

When you please God you will be taken away by God and not by death. Because the Bible says when you believe you shall not see death. 

A child of God is like a pyramid, and he has a white stone inside him/her which is a theophany. A church is not a society, to bury you. You go to church for to worship. 

Godâ€™s word is eternal and can only dwell in eternity. An eternal being cannot live in something that is not eternal. The word of God cannot survive in me if I do not have an eternal theophany. God does not force himself in the people, He comes to His place. That is why it says your body is the temple of God. He has prepared a place in you. When He comes to you, it is to the place that He has prepared for himself.
